# Café Sales Dashboard – Revenue & Customer Experience Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on building an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze café sales data with the objective of understanding revenue performance and customer experience. The dashboard is designed to support business stakeholders in making data-driven decisions related to pricing, promotions, staffing, and service improvement.

---

## Business Objective
The primary goal of this dashboard is to:
- Analyze overall revenue and sales trends
- Identify high-performing products and categories
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on net sales
- Understand customer experience through ratings and feedback
- Provide actionable insights for operational and strategic decision-making

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional café sales data including:
- Order details (Order ID, Date, Time, Order Type)
- Product information (Item Name, Category, Size, Quantity, Unit Price)
- Sales metrics (Total Sales, Net Sales, Discount %)
- Customer attributes (Age, Gender, Rating, Feedback)
- Operational details (Branch Name, Waiter Name)

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard includes the following KPIs:
- **Total Net Sales**
- **Total Orders**
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**
- **Average Customer Rating**
- **Percentage of Orders with Discount**

These KPIs provide a high-level summary of both financial performance and customer satisfaction.

---

## Dashboard Pages
### Page 1: Revenue & Growth
- Net Sales trend over time
- Sales distribution by product category
- Top-performing items by revenue
- Impact of discounts on net sales
- Interactive slicers for date, branch, category, and order type

### Page 2: Customer Experience & Operations
- Sales distribution by hour to identify peak periods
- Average customer rating by branch
- Relationship between sales and customer ratings
- Customer feedback analysis
- Operational slicers for branch, day, and order type

---

## Key Insights
- Beverage category contributes the highest share of total revenue
- Sales fluctuate over time, indicating seasonal and demand-based patterns
- Peak sales occur during evening hours, highlighting staffing and promotion opportunities
- Higher discounts do not result in proportional increases in revenue
- Customer ratings remain relatively consistent across branches

## Outcome
This project demonstrates the ability to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights using interactive dashboards. The final dashboard helps stakeholders understand revenue drivers, evaluate customer experience, and make informed business decisions.
